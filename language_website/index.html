FAQ

If you do not find the answers to your questions
Please do not hestitate to contact us, we would be more than happy to help!


What are the best language resources available online?

On our wesbite, we have regrouped the best language resources available for each of the languages offered. You can find the resources in the form of videos and/or vocabulary.
How can I get started with learning a new language?

The key is to start by setting clearly defined and achievable goals, using suitable resources available (suitable online resources you can find on our website) and praticing regularly.
What languages do you offer?

We offer 7 languages: Chinese, Dutch, English, French, Korean, Swahili and Urdu.
What are some common challenges faced by language learners?

Some common challenges include maintaining motivation, mastering pronounciation, understanding grammar rules and maintaining confidence in practicing speaking the language.
Is it possible to learn a language without taking formal classes?

Absolutely! Many people learn languages through self-study using online resources and books.
What are the benefits of learning a new language?

Benefits of learning a new language include improved cognitive function, better job opportunities, and the opportunity to learn about different cultures.
